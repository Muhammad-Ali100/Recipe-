KASURI MUTTON BIRYANI

INGREDIENTS

  • Boilled rice 1/2 kg
  • Mutton 1/2 kg
  • Tomatoes 1/2 kg
  • Fenugreek Seeds 2tsp
  • Cumin seeds 1tsp
  • Coriander whole 1tsp
  • Green cardamom 12
  • Tumeric powder 1tsp
  • salt 1tsp
  • Lemon 4
  • Black Pepercoms 1tsp
  • Star anis 2
  • Ginger garlic 2tsp
  • Onion 250gm
  • Bay leave 2
  • Oil 1cup
  • Red chilli 1-1/2tsp

METHOD

  1. Put water,ginger,garlic and red chilli in mutton and boil.
  2. Heat oil in pot and fry onion until brown.
  3. THen put boilled mutton,tomatoes,fenugreek seeds,coriander,cumin,cardamom,salt,black pepercom,star anise and bay leaves and roast on high flame.
  4. Then put boilled rice and simmer it for 20 minutes.
  5. It is ready.
